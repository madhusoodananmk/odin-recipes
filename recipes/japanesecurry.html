Japanese Curry

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil, or more as needed
  • 1 ¾ pounds beef chuck, cut into 2-inch cubes
  • 3 onions, quartered
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1 ½ teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper, or to taste (Optional) water to cover
  • 4 carrots, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 cube chicken bouillon (Optional)
  • 3 medium potatoes, cut into 3-inch chunks
  • 1 ½ (3.5 ounce) containers Japanese curry roux, or more to taste

Steps

  1. Step I
  2. Heat oil in a 6-quart pot over medium-high heat.
  3. Add beef and saute until brown, 5 to 7 minutes.
  4. Add onions and cook until starting to soften, about 3 minutes.
  5. Add ketchup and Worcestershire sauce.
  6. Stir to coat. Add cayenne pepper.
  7. Pour in water to cover mixture by 1 or 2 inches.
  8. Add carrots and bouillon.
  9. Step II
  10. Simmer, skimming fat off the surface of the broth as needed, for 30 minutes.
  11. Add potatoes. Stir in 1 package of curry roux and let dissolve;
  12. add remaining curry as needed to achieve desired thickness.
  13. Continue simmering until beef and vegetables are tender, about 30 minutes more.
